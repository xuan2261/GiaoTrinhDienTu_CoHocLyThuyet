# Mo rong ngan hang trac nghiem len 100 cau moi chuong

## Overview

Muc tieu: moi chapter co dung `100` cau trac nghiem chat luong, bam sat noi dung giao trinh hien tai, van chay offline qua `file://`, va co test gate de khong quay lai trang thai `50` cau hoac bundle cu.

Hien trang:

| File | Count | Section distribution |
|---|---:|---|
| `data/quiz-ch1.json` | 50 | I:8, II:8, III:7, IV:5, V:5, VI:5, VII:6, VIII:6 |
| `data/quiz-ch2.json` | 50 | I:10, II:8, III:5, IV:6, V:8, VI:5, VII:8 |
| `data/quiz-ch3.json` | 50 | I:4, II:8, III:6, IV:5, V:10, VI:7, VII:10 |

Target:

| Chapter | I | II | III | IV | V | VI | VII | Total |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Ch1 - Tinh hoc | 12 | 9 | 9 | 18 | 32 | 5 | 15 | 100 |
| Ch2 - Dong hoc | 15 | 11 | 10 | 14 | 24 | 11 | 15 | 100 |
| Ch3 - Dong luc hoc | 10 | 27 | 11 | 9 | 14 | 14 | 15 | 100 |

Decision: Ch1 JSON hien co `section: "VIII"` cho bai tap tinh toan, nhung manifest DOCX chi co VII. Normalize toan bo `VIII -> VII`; khong giu legacy `VIII` trong quiz data moi.

## Non-Goals

- Khong thay doi `js/quiz.js` neu 100 cau render on dinh.
- Khong them backend, database, CMS, hoac runtime bundler.
- Khong sua tay `js/pages.js`; chi regenerate bang `tools/bundle_pages.py`.
- Khong sua noi dung DOCX goc neu chi bo sung quiz JSON.

## Success Criteria

- `data/quiz-ch1.json`, `data/quiz-ch2.json`, `data/quiz-ch3.json` moi file dung `100` cau.
- Moi cau co `question`, 4 `options`, `correct` trong `0..3`, `section`, `feedbackCorrect`, `feedbackWrong`.
- Khong co duplicate exact/normalized question trong cung chapter.
- Section coverage dat target hoac co ly do ro trong report.
- Browser quiz page render `100` cards o all mode va `10` cards o random mode. Random mode giu 10 cau/luot.
- `js/pages.js` bundle khop JSON moi.
- `python tools\audit.py` pass.
