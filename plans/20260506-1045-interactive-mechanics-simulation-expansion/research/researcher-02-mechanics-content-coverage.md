# Mechanics Coverage Research

## Summary

Every chapter has interactive value. Highest learning impact: friction/self-locking, Coriolis/relative acceleration, differential equation motion, angular momentum, collision coefficient, and step-by-step problem solvers.

## Coverage Strategy

| Chapter | Current Strength | Main Gaps | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ch1 Tĩnh học | moment, couple, supports, centroid, parallelogram | ma sát, FBD builder, 3D force reduction, equation solver | P1 |
| Ch2 Động học | particle motion, natural coords, rotation, gears, rolling, velocity composition | Cartesian plots, acceleration composition/Coriolis, IC center, fixed-point rotation | P1 |
| Ch3 Động lực học | F=ma, Newton 3, momentum, energy, collision | differential equation lab, forward/inverse problem, angular momentum, impulse plots | P1 |

## Activity Types

| Type | Best For | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Simulation | continuous parameter exploration | friction cone, projectile |
| Animation | process/time evolution | rolling, collision |

| Step checker | problem solving | support reactions |
